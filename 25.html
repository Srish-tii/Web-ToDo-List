INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Welcome to your own web toDo-list app to finally keep a track of your daily activities efficiently!
  2. Click on the "Add new Todo" to add a new toDo to the list.
  3. Hover over any added toDo for the trash icon to appear. Click on this trashcan icon to permanently delete the toDo.
  4. On completing any task, you can also temporarily strike through a toDo by simply clicking on it. For the task to appear pending again, click on the striked off toDo.
  5. Click on the icon on the top bar to hide/unhide the input box.
  6. Add as many toDos as you want and enjoy!
